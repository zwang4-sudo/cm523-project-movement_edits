About The Movement Edit

A collection to the dance spaces Boston has to offer, for dancers of every background and style.

What Is This?

The Movement Edit is a hand-picked collection of eight dance studios in the Greater Boston area. From the polished stages of Boston Ballet to the raw energy of Phunk Phenomenon, each entry was chosen for its unique culture, accessibility, and commitment to the craft.

About the Curator

This project was created as part of CM523 at Boston University. It grew out of a personal love for dance.
