Find Your Rhythm

An artistic review of the top 8 dance spaces in the Boston area for grace, grit, and growth.

Classical

Boston Ballet

Location: South End, Newton, Marblehead.
Genres: Classical Ballet, Contemporary, Barre.
Fees: $20–$25 drop-in.
A world-class institution offering professional-grade instruction for all ages through their Adult Dance Program.

Visit Studio
Classical

Arrais Ballet

Location: Needham, MA.
Genres: Classical Ballet, Pointe, Contemporary.
Fees: $25 per class.
Founded by Paulo Arrais, this studio offers refined technique and personalized artistry in an intimate setting.

Visit Studio
Urban

Phunk Phenomenon

Location: Everett, MA.
Genres: Hip-Hop, Popping, Locking, Breaking.
Fees: $15–$20 drop-in.
A "non-traditional" complex famous for ABDC, focusing on the authentic culture and five elements of Hip-Hop.

Visit Studio
Contemporary / Jazz

Urbanity Dance

Location: South End, Boston.
Genres: Contemporary, Modern, Jazz.
Fees: $20 drop-in (New student deals available).
Bridges the gap between professional art and community engagement with a highly supportive atmosphere.

Visit Studio
Jazz

The Dance Complex

Location: Central Square, Cambridge.
Genres: African, Flamenco, Vogue, Tap, Ballet.
Fees: $18–$22 per class.
A massive, historic hub acting as a clearinghouse for independent teachers and diverse global styles.

Visit Studio
Ballroom

Firebeat Ballroom

Location: Waltham, MA.
Genres: Waltz, Tango, Salsa, Cha Cha.
Fees: $25 drop-in / $125 Private.
A sleek, upscale studio specializing in high-level competitive training and elegant social ballroom dance.

Visit Studio
Latin

Salsa y Control

Location: Allston & Cambridge.
Genres: Salsa (On 1) and Bachata.
Fees: $20 drop-in / $90 for 5-week sessions.
One of New England’s most respected Salsa companies, known for emphasizing technique and "flavor."

Visit Studio
Street

Boston Dance Studios

Location: Downtown (Friend St).
Genres: Commercial Hip-Hop, Street Jazz, Heels.
Fees: $22 per class.
The premier hub for professional-level street styles and commercial choreography in the heart of the city.
